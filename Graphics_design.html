Graphic Desinging

Graphic designs are found in newspapers, billboards, posters, books and even on websites but they persist in varying degree depending on the context. Graphics has the potential to attract customers to any business as they are an effective form of advertisement. In general, graphics have a tendency to deliver a message in a stronger fashion when compared to plain text. You may ask how it is so. It is because a large percentage of people can easily remember a visual representation of a specific subject when compared to its auditory or textual representation.
